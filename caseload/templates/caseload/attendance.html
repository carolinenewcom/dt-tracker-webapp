Attendance

Developmental Therapy is a program to help parents support their children in the developmental milestones they are delayed in. Funding for Developmental Therapy is contingent upon maintaining a 75% or higher attendance rate. If a family consistently falls below a 75% attendance rate, they are subject to program dismissal. In order to ensure that families are secure in their program standing, it is crucial to track attendance percentages.
