Sessions

At the end of each month, Developmental Therapists are required to input attendance information regarding their monthly sessions for each child on their caseload. For each month, Developmental Therapists will total their number of completed sessions, excused absences, and unexcused absences. This data will be used to determine attendance percentages.

To view a child's past monthly session logs, enter a child's first name, last name, or ID number.
